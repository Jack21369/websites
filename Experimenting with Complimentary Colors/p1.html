This is the Website with Complimentary Colors

You will not find much at this website besides Bob Ross quotes, fun facts about color, and a visually appealing color scheme composed of grey, orange, light-blue, and white.

Visually Appealing Color Scheme

Choosing complimentary colors is quite difficult as their are endless combinations that can be made to produce a pallete that is appealing to the eye. Too much contrast results in eye strain, too little makes it hard to view the website. That is why I decided to choose colors that are not too contrasting, but are easily visible to the eye.

Fun Facts About Color

Colors heavily influence our thinking and psychology. For example, reds and yellows are known to make someone hungry. Too much yellow is also known to cause dizziness. Pink can be a calming color. This is why choosing color schemes are a significant part of any industry, whether that be food, clothing technology, etc.

Red is also known as the most powerful and aggressive color. There are about 10 million shades of color in the world.
