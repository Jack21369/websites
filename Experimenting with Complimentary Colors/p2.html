Fun Facts About Water

Water is made of 2 hydrogen molecules and 1 oxygen molecule. 97% of the world's water supply cannot be used by humans (like the ocean), 2% is frozen, and 1% of the water supply is usable by humans. Humans can live much longer without food (around a month) compared to living without water, which is around a week.

Water expands when it is frozen. Humans are around 70% water. Water can transform into the 3 states of matter; gas, solid, and liquid.
